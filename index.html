About This Story

What Love Isn’t follows the emotional journey of Justin, a young man learning how to heal after surviving abuse, betrayal, fear, and emotional trauma. Through friendship, faith, boundaries, and genuine care, Justin slowly discovers that love was never meant to harm him.

This story was written for survivors, for those rebuilding trust, and for anyone searching for peace after painful seasons of life.

Healing

Healing begins when fear no longer controls the heart. This story explores emotional restoration, safety, and reclaiming life after trauma.

Boundaries

Boundaries are not walls built from bitterness. Healthy boundaries become protection that allows growth, trust, and peace to flourish again.

Hope

Even after abuse and heartbreak, hope remains possible. Freedom, joy, and safe love can still be found beyond the storm.

“Love does not have to hurt. Love can heal, protect, restore, and remain.”
