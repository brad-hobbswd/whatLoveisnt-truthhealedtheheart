Dedication

For every heart that survived what never should have happened.

This story is dedicated to every person who questioned their worth after being mistreated, abandoned, manipulated, silenced, or emotionally broken by those who claimed to love them. For every survivor learning that healing remains possible. For every heart rebuilding trust after fear. For every soul discovering that boundaries are not cruelty and peace does not require apology. For those who stayed alive through the darkest nights and still carried hope somewhere deep within themselves. May this story remind you that genuine love protects, restores, listens, values, and never destroys the soul. You deserved safe love all along.
Bradley Hobbs
