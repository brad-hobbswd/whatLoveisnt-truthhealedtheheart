Dedication

For every soul learning that healing and safe love are still possible.

To every soul who has endured the crushing weight of abuse, betrayal, and heartbreak, yet chose not to surrender to despair.

To the quiet survivors rebuilding shattered trust, rediscovering peace, and learning once again that love should never come with fear.

To the faithful friends, protectors, and compassionate hearts who remain steady through the darkest nights; your presence brings hope where loneliness once lived.

And to every person holding onto the belief that healing still exists, that freedom can rise from pain, and that joy and safe love are still possible after devastation.

This story belongs to you.

Bradley Hobbs
