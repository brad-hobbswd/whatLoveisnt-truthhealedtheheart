Prayer of Commitment

A prayer for healing, restoration, freedom, and walking forward in peace.

Heavenly Father,

Thank You for being our refuge, our strength, and our safe place when the storms of life threaten to overwhelm us. Thank You for walking with us through pain, through fear, and through the nights that seem endless.

Lord, I lift up every heart reading this book. Heal the wounds that have been inflicted, restore the broken places, and help us to reclaim the life You intended for us. Teach us to set healthy boundaries, to trust again, and to walk in freedom without fear.

Father, pour Your peace into our hearts. Surround us with people who reflect Your love, are kind, and safe. Give us courage to let go of the past, wisdom to recognize what is harmful, and faith to believe that love can heal and uplift.

Lord Jesus, I commit to living in Your truth. Help me to forgive; not to forget, but to release the hold of pain and hurt so that I may live fully and love freely. Let Your presence guide my steps and guard my heart.

May every survivor of abuse or betrayal know Your comfort and protection. May they find hope, restoration, and joy in Your promises. And may their lives be a testimony of Your faithfulness, love, and healing power.

In the name of Jesus, who is our one true God and Savior, I pray.

Amen.
