Prayer of Commitment

A prayer for healing, peace, restoration, safety, wisdom, and learning how to walk forward without fear controlling the heart.

Jesus,

Thank You for carrying me through every painful season that tried to destroy my peace, my identity, and my hope.

Thank You for remaining near during moments when fear, confusion, rejection, manipulation, emotional pain, and exhaustion felt overwhelming.

Today I choose healing.

I choose honesty over silence.

I choose peace over fear.

I choose truth over emotional confusion.

Help me recognize healthy love clearly.

Teach me that genuine care never requires self destruction, emotional control, shame, intimidation, or fear in order to survive.

Strengthen my heart wherever trauma still lingers.

Restore every broken place within me that pain tried to silence.

Teach me healthy boundaries without guilt.

Surround me with safe people who protect peace, respect honesty, value truth, and encourage healing.

Remove every voice that continually attempts to manipulate, condemn, control, or diminish the worth You placed within me.

Help me walk forward without carrying shame from seasons I survived.

Remind me daily that my value never depended upon the approval of unhealthy people.

Fill my heart with wisdom, peace, stability, rest, and courage for the future ahead.

Thank You for healing me slowly, faithfully, and completely through Your love and mercy.

In the name of Jesus, Amen.
