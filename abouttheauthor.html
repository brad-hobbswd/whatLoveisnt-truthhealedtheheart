About the Author

Stories written with hope, healing, faith, and the belief that restoration remains possible.

Bradley Hobbs is a passionate writer who believes in the power of storytelling to bring healing, understanding, and hope. Drawing from real emotions and deep empathy, Brad writes with authenticity and compassion for those who have faced pain, trauma, and the struggle to rebuild trust.

His work reflects a heart for restoration and a belief that love, when it is true, safe, and grounded in God, has the power to transform even the deepest wounds. Through his writing, Brad aims to remind readers that no matter how dark the past has been, freedom and peace are possible.

When he is not writing, Brad enjoys quiet mornings, meaningful conversations, and moments of reflection that inspire his next story. He continues to share messages of resilience and hope, encouraging others to believe that healing is not just a dream, but a promise fulfilled through faith, courage, and love.
