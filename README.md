# What Love Isn’t  
## The Truth That Healed the Heart

A story of survival, healing, trust, boundaries, and discovering that real love never destroys the soul.

---

## About

*What Love Isn’t: The Truth That Healed the Heart* follows Justin’s journey through heartbreak, trauma, recovery, friendship, faith, and rediscovering what healthy love truly looks like.

This story explores:

- Healing after abuse and betrayal  
- Rebuilding trust  
- Emotional boundaries  
- Recovery from fear and trauma  
- Friendship and safe relationships  
- Faith and restoration  
- Learning that love should never require fear
